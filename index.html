About this project

The ongoing war, humanitarian and economic crisis in Ukraine left many families in villages near the front line struggling to survive. Our project seeks to alleviate hardships of most vulnerable families by providing the basic necessities they need to survive.

Each food box contains non-perishable food items (grains, pasta, canned goods), basic hygiene products (soap, toothpaste), and a set of batteries.

Your contribution helps us buy, pack, and deliver these boxes to towns and villages in eastern and southern Ukraine.

Together, we can make a difference and provide much-needed assistance to those who need it most.

About UkraineNow

UkraineNow

Our focus is on providing humanitarian aid to Ukraine, evacuation of women, children, and vulnerable people from the war zone, and improving living circumstances of Ukrainians affected by war.

We also frequently partner with organizations and volunteers in Ukraine to help them make their impact in a matter of weeks instead of months.

Since the beginning of the full-scale Russian invasion, UkraineNow has brought together more than 2,000 volunteers across 20 different nationalities and a variety of languages. Additionally, UkraineNow was supported by multiple companies.

UkraineNow is an initiative of a US-based 501c3, IRS-approved non-profit organization Radical Philanthropies, created in 2020 by Artur Kiulian.
