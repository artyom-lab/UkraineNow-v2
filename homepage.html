FAQ
What is the story behind PatronBadge?
Patron Badge is a project started by three Ukrainian volunteers to reward people for their support of good causes.It is currently in a beta version with more features coming soon!
How does it work?
For every donation via PatronBadge you get a cool digital badge to which you can add your name or alias. This badge exists as a record in our database and as a unique page on our website.You can share this badge on social media to show your support and encourage your followers to donate to the cause.You always have an option of keeping your badge anonymous.
Is donating to a charity via Patron Badge different from donating to it directly?
Not at all - it works exactly the same! We connect the charity accounts directly to our payment forms so that your money hits their account immediately. It never passes through Patron Badge accounts and we never charge any fees from any donations.However, we do record the details of the transaction (e.g. your name, email, donation amount) to be able to issue you a corresponding badge and provide a good user experience. This data is confidential and is shared only with the charity to which you donated.
Where can I find the list of all my badges?
After every donation, we send you a link to the page on our website that contains your badge. We recommend saving or bookmarking this page.We are currently working on user profiles that will contain all the badges. Sign up to our newsletter below not to miss this feature!If you lost the link or need any other help please contact us at team@patronbadge.com.
Who can register?
Anyone with a valid payment method can donate to charities via Patron Badge.As for organizations, we currently work with registered non-profits in the US and Ukraine. Contact us via the form below to get in touch with us and learn more.
